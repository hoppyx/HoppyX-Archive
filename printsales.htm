Print Sales Catalogue

 

 

B&W exhibition prints may be viewed at The Photographers' Gallery, Print Sales Dept, 5 & 8 Gt Newport St, London WC2H 7HY. Tel: (020) 7831 1772 to check opening times.

Period: 1958-1966. Main subject areas are:

- Rock & roll (Beatles, Stones, Marianne Faithfull, blues artistes)


- Jazz (Armstrong, Ellington, Monk, Brubeck, Peterson, Ornette Coleman, Dexter Gordon, NY avant garde)


- Politics & peace (Martin Luther King, CND, Aldermaston, MalcolmX, James Baldwin, Bertrand Russell, Linus Pauling, demonstrations)


- Poverty (street children, graffiti, poor housing)


- Drugs (the joint factory, LSD meets CND, dope various)


- Nudes (the ineffably beautiful Gala, at home)


- Pastoral (love and children in the park, Speaker's corner)


- Low life (tattooist, prostitute, rubber fetishist, sex workers)


- Bikers (bikers at the Ace café, now gay ikons)


for sizes and prices <printsales@photonet.org.uk>
