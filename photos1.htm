Photos

From the late 1950's to 1965, I was a London-based photojournalist. Then, engulfed in psychedelia, the black and white negatives were forgotten and all the prints lost, stolen or strayed. Recently the negs were rediscovered and prints are now selling to collectors following a couple of successful 1-man shows. Pix are also available for use in TV, press, books etc. Full cataloging will take months if not years, and the website is being regularly updated. I am indebted to Adam Blake, Evelyn Wilson and Addie Elliott for their encouragement.
