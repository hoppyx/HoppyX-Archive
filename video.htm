VIDEO, 1970-90: Print & Tape archives

 

Hoppy was one of the pioneers of the international independent video movement from 1969 onwards, via TVX Video, London New Arts Lab and, with Sue Hall, Fantasy Factory Video, a leading arts post house in 80's London.

- Rare Print collections. A few sets only of the main periodicals are available for sale as a unique research resource for institutions and collectors: Journal of the Centre for Advanced TV Studies (JCATS) 1972-80, Video Extra 1976-80, Independent Video 1980-90. Prices on application.

- Print Archive. Reference sets of Radical Software (NY) and Challenge for Change (NFB Canada) may be inspected in person, by appointment, please enquire.

- Video programme sampler. A 60 minute video containing 15 clips from programmes made 1970-90 is available, enquiries welcomed. A full catalogue of this unique video archive awaits the resources to relubricate and transfer 100's of original tapes. Let us know if you have any ideas on this !!
